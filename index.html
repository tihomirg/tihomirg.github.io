Short Biography

Tihomir Gvero holds a PhD degree in Computer Science from EPFL, Switzerland, and a Master’s degree also in Computer Science from the University of Belgrade, Serbia. During the PhD studies he implemented Java and Scala assistance and testing tools. In 2010, he did an internship in Microsoft, Redmond, USA where he improved Microsoft Visual Studio (in particular a tool for automated software testing). In 2007, he did an internship at UIUC, USA where he worked on automated testing. Since joining Itecor, he has been developing a web portal and a desktop application for MSF.
